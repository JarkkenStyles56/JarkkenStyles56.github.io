A little bit about me...



I've always lived up north, and in smaller towns. I was born in the upper peninsula of Michigan, AKA "Da UP", and my family and I moved to a tiny town in North Dakota called Ellendale ( Population: 1,200 during the school year) when I was 5 years old.. Lived there all the way through school excepting a small stint in an even small town in South Dakota called Warner which sits at about 480 people currently. It was in Ellendale in 6th grade when I met my wife, we started dating the summer before our senior year. Although that was when her and her family left for the east coast. We were long distance for about 3 1/2 years before we both ended up in Helena, MT. She moved here to live with her grandma, and I moved here to be with her. We got married June 15th, 2018 and we're expecting our first child in February of 2021.
